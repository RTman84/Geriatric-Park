1. Introduction

Welcome to Geriatric Park ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you play Geriatric Park (the "Game") accessible at our website.

By using the Game, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Game.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Location Data: With your permission, we access your device's GPS location to power the map-based gameplay (spawning items, Elders, and structures near you). Location data is used in real time and is not stored on our servers.
  • Game Save Data: Your game progress (level, inventory, pension balance, etc.) is saved locally in your browser's localStorage. We do not transmit this data to our servers.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymized data about how you interact with the Game (e.g., features used, session duration) to improve the experience.

3. Advertising

Geriatric Park displays advertisements served by Google AdSense, including rewarded video ads. Google may use cookies and device identifiers to serve personalized ads based on your interests and browsing activity.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings .

For more information on how Google uses data when you use our site, visit: How Google uses data from sites that use Google services .

4. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies for the following purposes:

  • Saving your game preferences and settings
  • Serving and measuring advertisements via Google AdSense
  • Analyzing game performance and usage patterns

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect some Game features.

5. Third-Party Services

The Game uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdSense – advertising: Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini API – AI-powered game features: Google AI Terms
  • Vercel – website hosting: Vercel Privacy Policy

6. Children's Privacy

Geriatric Park is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Data Retention

Since game data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage or using the "Clear Save & Reset" option within the Game. We do not retain personal data on our servers.

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Withdraw consent for location access at any time via your device settings
